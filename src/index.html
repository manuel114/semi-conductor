How It Works

Revenue Maestro is an interactive experience that lets you conduct your GTM team through your browser. Move your arms to control the tempo, volume, and coordination of your revenue operations.

It uses Tensorflow.js, a machine learning library that works in the browser, to map out your movements through your webcam. As you conduct, an algorithm orchestrates different GTM functions - from Sales Cadence to Marketing Signals - creating a symphony of aligned revenue operations.

This experience was inspired by Rosalyn Santa Elena, VP of GTM Operations at SingleStore, and her insights on "Scale Faster: The Rise of Revenue Orchestration Platforms."

Built on top of Semi-Conductor, an AI experiment by Google Creative Lab, Sydney. Customized by Manuel Martinez Chamorro from www.getvera.ai.
